# Back to Bottom

A 2D platformer where you play as a squishy, bouncy soft body slime. It's a combination of angry bird(movement) and getting over it(Main Goal).

## Cool Features

- **Soft-Body Physics** on the player.

- Your exact coordinates are saved when you press esc Key and quit the game. Click continue game if you want to continue from where you left your game.

- As you get higher or lower in the world, the background gently shifts color to match your altitude.

## Controls

- Left Mouse Button -  Click + Drag to Aim.
- Release Left Mouse Button to Launch!
- ESC key - Saves your progress and returns to the Main Menu.

## AI Usage
- Used AI for aimdots and some bug fixes!
- Used for parallax backgrounds!
